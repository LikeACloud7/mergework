# Code of Conduct

MergeWork contributors are expected to work professionally and in good faith.

- Be respectful and specific in technical discussion.
- Avoid harassment, threats, spam, and abusive behavior.
- Do not submit fake work, duplicate noise, or fabricated payout claims.
- Respect private security handling rules.

Maintainers may close, reject, or block submissions that disrupt the project or
put users at risk.
