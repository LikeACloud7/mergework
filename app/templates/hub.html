Future path

MRWK starts as a native project coin on the MergeWork ledger. The ledger is designed for future public snapshots, bridges, or onchain claims if the network grows enough to support them.

Accounts and payouts

Accepted work is paid to a native MRWK ledger account such as github:ramimbo. That account id is the current MRWK address inside this ledger. External wallet sending is not active in v0; the next step is a signed transfer or wallet module.
